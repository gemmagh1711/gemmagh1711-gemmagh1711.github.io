About me

Hi my name is Gemma Ghalloub and I am a a 22 years old pre-service teacher, studying at ACU Strathfield. I will Graduate in 2026 with a double degree: Bachelor of Education Secondary and a Bachelor of Arts (Design Technologies and Innovation). I will finish with a major in Food Technology and Design and Technology along with a minor in Textiles and Design as well as Computing.
When I am not teaching, I enjoy going to the beach, hanging out with friend and family, taking my 2 dogs on walks reading and swimming.

So far I have completed and passed 3 placements throughout my degree, all being 3 week blocks.

Oakhill Catholic College, Castle Hill

My first 2 placements were at the same school: Oakhill Catholic College, Castle Hill. Oakhill is an all boys school until year 11 where girls are welcomed. At this school I taught a range of students from years 7-12. I was placed into a range of different subject areas including Food Technology, Industrial Timber and Metal and Agriculture.

Mercy Catholic College, Chatswood

My third placement was at Mercy Catholic College, Chatwood. Mercy college is an all girls, catholic school. At this school I taught a range of students from years 9-11 in the food technology subject. Throughout this placement, I built confidence and many skills while also creating strong relationships with students. Here I also worked on developing classroom management skills.

Throughout all of these placement experiences, I have created in depth lesson plans which have assisted me in preparing and presenting high quality and engaging lessons to the students I was teaching. I used a range of different teaching approaches including project based learning activites and group work as well as specific individual tasks. Some activity examples include: creating a day in the life of a farmer video, group research tasks and literacy based lessons that work initially as a group then finish individually.
